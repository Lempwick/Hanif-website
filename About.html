About Me

Hi I'm Hanif!

Hanif “NIFFY” Brandy (stylized as HAN!F), who is also known as Her Majesty The King, is a multi-talented singer-songwriter from Oakland, California. As a producer, they mainly dabble in RnB, but their musical background ranges from classical to gospel and neo-soul to pop. This celestial being creates mind-blowing sonic experiences for the masses.
